ABC Technologies Company Policy

Refund Policy
----------------

Customers may request refunds within 30 days of purchase.

All refund requests require approval from a human supervisor.

Subscription Cancellation
----------------------------

Customers may cancel subscriptions at any time.

Cancellation requests require approval before processing.

Account Closure
-------------------

Account closure requests require identity verification and supervisor approval.

Compensation Requests
--------------------------

Compensation requests are reviewed individually by management.

Escalation Requests
-----------------------

Requests for management escalation are reviewed manually.
